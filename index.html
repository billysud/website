Systems

Windows 10 → 11 Remote Upgrade Script

With Microsoft ending Windows 10 support, I wrote a custom script to silently upgrade compatible devices to Windows 11 in the background — no takeover of user machines required. Audited hardware inventory to flag ineligible devices, handled full data and settings migrations for those users, and achieved a 100% success rate across the entire organization.

Infrastructure

ShoreTel → RingCentral VoIP Migration

RingCentral support told us it was impossible to migrate legacy ShoreTel desk phones and conference phones to their platform. I investigated firmware-level alternatives and cracked it — flashing new firmware onto the old hardware to point at RingCentral, saving the company from purchasing entirely new devices. Also configured an ATA adapter for the fax machine, avoiding recurring subscription costs.

VoIPFirmwareNetwork Config
Automation

SharePoint Offboarding Export

When a subsidiary ceased operations, I automated the export of their entire SharePoint — a process complicated by large data volumes, security permissions, and nested dependencies. Built a system where all that's needed is to set permissions and specify the site. Data was exported, shipped to the relevant contact, and confirmed complete — eliminating what would have been a days-long manual effort.
